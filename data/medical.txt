Fever:
Fever is a temporary increase in body temperature, often due to infection.
Symptoms: sweating, chills, headache, muscle aches.
Advice: Drink plenty of fluids, take rest, and use paracetamol if needed.

Cold:
Common cold is a viral infection of the nose and throat.
Symptoms: runny nose, sneezing, sore throat.
Advice: Stay hydrated, take rest, and use steam inhalation.

Headache:
Headaches can occur due to stress, dehydration, or lack of sleep.
Symptoms: pain in head, pressure, sensitivity to light.
Advice: Drink water, rest in a quiet place, and avoid stress.

Diabetes:
Diabetes is a chronic disease that affects blood sugar levels.
Symptoms: increased thirst, frequent urination, fatigue.
Advice: Maintain healthy diet, exercise regularly, monitor sugar levels.

High Blood Pressure:
Also known as hypertension, it increases risk of heart disease.
Symptoms: often no symptoms, sometimes headaches or dizziness.
Advice: Reduce salt intake, exercise regularly, monitor BP.

Stomach Pain:
Can be caused by indigestion, gas, or infection.
Symptoms: abdominal pain, bloating, nausea.
Advice: Eat light food, avoid spicy items, stay hydrated.
